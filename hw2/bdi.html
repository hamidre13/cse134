The <bdi> tag

  • <bdi> element stands for Bi-Directional Isolation Element
  • <bdi> element isolates a string of text that maight be formatted in a different direction then the text surrounding it
  • <bdi> is most typically use to isolate arabic words(right to left directional) surrounded by English or other left-to-right directional language
  • <bdi>

This arabic word إيان is automatically displayed right-to-left when enclose in <bdi> tag.
