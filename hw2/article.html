The <article> tag

  • <article> tag represent a self-contained composition in a document
  • <article> contains pieces of information that can be and is intended to be independently distrubuted
  • Examples of such information includes:
    • Magazine or newspaper article
    • Blog post
    • Forum post

Example

The following is a piece of information within a <article> tag, with the header "Corgi"

Corgi

The Welsh corgi is a small type of herding dog that originated in Wales.
