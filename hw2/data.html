The <data> tag

  • <data> elements links a term or content with a machine readable translation
  • <data> is use with the attributes value which defines the translation of the term enclosed
  • <data> can be use to translate food product term with its numeric barcode value, making such term machine-readable

Example

The followings are a list of ingredients needed to make Kale salad. Each item has a machine-redable barcode associated with it, defined by the <data> tag

  • Kale
  • Quinoa
  • Lemon
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Lemon
