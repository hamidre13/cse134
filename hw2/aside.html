The <aside> tag

  • <aside> is used to group a piece of information that are only slightly related to the main content of the container its in or the page.
  • Example of content grouped in <aside> includes:
    • Definitions of a certain word
    • Biography of the author
    • other related link of the blog
  • <aside> can also be used to group related advertisement on a page
  • <aside> grouped information that are often represented as inserts or sidebars

Example

Because the description of the dog breed Welsh Corgi is not directly related to the main content(Jennifer Aniston loosing her dog), it is grouped inside the <aside> tags
