The <address> tag

  • <address> provides the contact information of the author/owner of a document or article
  • <address> italicized the text within the tag
  • <address> within a <body> tag usually represents the contact information of the document's author
  • <address> within an <article> tag usually represents the contact information of the article's author

Example

This is my contact information.
858-123-4567
9500 Gilman Dr., San Diego, CA
<address>
This is my contact information.<br>
858-123-4567<br>
9500 Gilman Dr., San Diego, CA<br>
<address>
