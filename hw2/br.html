The <br> tag

  • <br> stands for break
  • <br> produces a line break in text, similar to a newline character
  • <br> is useful for writing a poem or address content where many line breaks are required
  • <br> does not need a closing tag </br>, simply type <br> when line break is needed

Example

<br> are used in the following poem to create line breaks, while still containing within the same <p>

Welsh Corgi

Shih Tzus are puffy,
Yorkies are bait,
Huskys are picky,
But Corgis are GREAT!
